Paris

Take a visit to the town of Montmartre. It is a charming neighborhood with beautiful views and many cafes. A few spots are also seen in the show “Emily in Paris”! The history of Montmartre is fascinating as many artists gravitated to this neighborhood. There is also the Basilica of the Sacre-Coeur at the top of the hilly neighborhood that you can enter and see.

Make sure to check out the Eifel Tower. Take some beatiful pitures and enjoy the great architecture. This is a very unique piece that no one should miss.

Paris is a beautiful and charming city with lots to see and delicious food. You must visit The Louvre. See gorgeous sculptures and the Mona Lisa. This tour is interesting as there is lots of history to be told throughout the museum.

Go to La Jacobine for THE BEST authentic French food in Paris. This is a very small restaurant but has the most amazing French dishes. Do not miss out on the foie gras and the French onion soup. Make sure to make a reservation as this small spot is very popular.Also, visit the Jewish Neighborhood where you will see many small stores and shops. This area has a lot of history and some great food. Enjoy the best falafel sandwich from L'As du Falafel. Go into the oldest and smallest synagogue and see just how well Jewish people hid their places of worship. At the end of the neighborhood visit the Holocaust Museum to see the history of it in Paris
