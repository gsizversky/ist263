Venice


Venice is a beautiful city that is surrounded by water. There are many things to see and do that you don't want to miss out on. When going to Venice definitely take a gondola ride. Sit and enjoy the ride and views while listening to some history and music by the rower. It is a fun and different experience that is great for all ages!


When in Venice, also take a trip to the Burano and Murano Islands. In Murano take a tour of the glass making factory and see the beautiful pieces that are made. Then head to Burano where each home is a different color. It is a fun and bright island great for pictures and just to walk around. Enjoy lunch or dinner at Trattoria al Gatto Nero for some authentic Italian cuisine while visiting.

Another great tour is the tour with locals in Venice. This is a tour of more local spots that are less touristy. During my tour I walked through the Jewish Ghetto, saw more residential living, and tried a local coffee shop where no one spoke English. It made me fall in love with the city as I saw charming spots that aren't commercialized.
